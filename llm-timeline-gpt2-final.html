LLM Architecture Timeline

Key architectural milestones · 2017 – Apr 2026

Date Model / Org Params / Architecture Key Innovation
Jun 2017 TransformerGoogle Brain —Encoder-Decoder Attention "Attention Is All You Need" — foundational architecture every LLM descends from.
Jun 2018 GPT-1OpenAI 117MDecoder-only Transformer Introduced the decoder-only pretrain-then-finetune paradigm; showed unsupervised pretraining on text transfers well to downstream tasks.
Feb 2019 GPT-2OpenAI 1.5BDecoder-only Transformer Scaled GPT-1's recipe; sparked mainstream AI awareness through its infamous staged release over safety concerns; open-sourced in full on GitHub.
Oct 2019 RMSNormZhang & Sennrich —Normalization layer Dropped LayerNorm's re-centering step while preserving scaling stability; later became standard in LLaMA-style decoder LLMs.
Feb 2020 SwiGLUShazeer (Google) —Activation function Replaced ReLU/GeLU in FFN layers with a gated variant; better gradient flow; later adopted by PaLM, LLaMA, and most modern decoder models.
May 2020 GPT-3OpenAI 175BDense decoder + pre-norm Demonstrated emergent few-shot in-context learning at scale; introduced pre-normalisation as a training stability technique.
Jun 2020 GShardGoogle 600BCoarse sparse MoE, top-k routing First to scale MoE to 600B parameters via top-k routing; established the blueprint DeepSeek later refined. Note: Shazeer et al.'s Jan 2017 Sparse MoE paper introduced the expert routing concept this builds on.
Apr 2021 RoPESu et al. —Positional encoding Rotary positional embeddings; hybrid absolute/relative encoding that generalises better to longer sequences than learned absolute positions.
Mar 2022 ChinchillaDeepMind 70BDense Transformer Compute-optimal scaling: more tokens on a smaller model beats a bigger undertrained model at the same compute budget. Rewrote scaling law intuitions.
Mar 2022 InstructGPT / RLHFOpenAI 1.3B – 175BRLHF (SFT + RM + PPO) Introduced the three-stage RLHF pipeline — supervised finetuning, reward modelling, PPO — that turned raw pretrained LLMs into usable instruction-following assistants.
May 2022 FlashAttentionDao et al. —IO-aware exact attention Rewrote attention to be memory-efficient and fast via tiled IO; unlocked practical long-context training and became the de-facto attention kernel.
Nov 2022 ChatGPTOpenAI GPT-3.5 classRLHF Chat Assistant Wrapped an RLHF-tuned LLM in a conversational web interface. Not a base architectural breakthrough, but it proved the commercial viability of chat-aligned models and triggered the global generative AI boom.
Feb 2023 LLaMA 1Meta 7B – 65BDense + RoPE / SwiGLU / RMSNorm / FlashAttention Synthesised the standard modern decoder recipe by combining RoPE, SwiGLU, RMSNorm, and FlashAttention; proved data quality beats raw scale; sparked the open-source LLM explosion.
Mar 2023 GPT-4 leak OpenAI ~1.8T total · ~280B activeReported: coarse MoE, top-2 of 16 Major capability milestone. Architecture figures (1.8T / 16 experts / top-2) are from leaks reported Jul 2023, not an official release; validated and accelerated the industry's MoE direction.
Jul 2023 LLaMA 2Meta 7B – 70BDense + GQA Added RLHF-based chat alignment; introduced Grouped-Query Attention (GQA) for faster inference at larger scales.
Dec 2023 Mixtral 8×7B open Mistral AI 46.7B total · 12.9B activeCoarse MoE, top-2 of 8 experts First open-weight MoE with broad adoption (11 Dec 2023); showed sparse routing could beat dense models at equal active-parameter cost.
May 2024 DeepSeek V2DeepSeek 236B total · 21B activeFine-grained MoE + MLA Invented MLA (compressed KV cache) and DeepSeekMoE — fine-grained experts with shared expert isolation.
Jul 2024 LLaMA 3.1Meta 405BDense + 128K context Released 23 Jul 2024. First open model genuinely competitive with top closed models; proved open dense could match closed at frontier scale.
Dec 2024 DeepSeek V3 open DeepSeek 671B total · 37B activeMoE + FP8 training + MTP Auxiliary-loss-free load balancing; FP8 training; multi-token prediction; ~$6M training cost — redefined efficiency expectations.
Sep 2024 OpenAI o1 OpenAI 671B total · 37B activeMoE + FP8 training + MTP First model to replace pre-training scaling with test-time compute scaling; uses reinforcement learning to produce extended internal chain-of-thought before answering. Introduced a new reasoning paradigm that directly inspired DeepSeek R1.
Jan 2025 DeepSeek R1 open DeepSeek 671B total · 37B activeV3 arch + pure RL post-training First open model to match o1-level reasoning using pure reinforcement learning, with no supervised finetuning warm-up.
Jul 2025 Kimi K2 open Moonshot AI 1T total · 32B activeMoE + MLA + MuonClip 1T-scale open MoE from Moonshot AI; used MuonClip (Muon optimiser variant) for training stability at trillion-parameter scale.
Apr 2026 Kimi K2.6 open Moonshot AI 1T total · 32B activeMoE + MLA + MuonClip, 384 experts, 256K ctx Released 20 Apr 2026. Expanded to 384 experts, 256K context; agent-swarm workflows with up to 300 parallel sub-agents across 4,000 coordinated steps.
